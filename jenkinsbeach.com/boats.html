Enjoy sightseeing on Green Lake in one of our clean, safety-equipped rentals from our fleet of fishing boats, row boats, canoes, kayaks, paddle boats or pontoon boats.

We rent by the hour, full day, or week. Reservations are strongly suggested, especially for that early morning fishing expedition.

Rentals include life vests, paddles, oars and a map of the lake.

Launching facilities exist for those with their own boats. Boat launch fee is $5.00.

 

Canoeing can be a quiet sport or get two full of young men and it can become a race!
